====== virgin ======


===== Definition: =====

A virgin is a woman who has never had sexual relations.

    * The prophet Isaiah said that the Messiah would be born from a virgin.
    * Mary was a virgin when she was pregnant with Jesus. He did not have a human father.
    * Some languages may have a term that is a polite way of referring to a virgin. (See: [[:bxu:ta:vol2:translate:figs_euphemism|Euphemism]])

(See also: [[:bxu:obe:kt:christ]], [[:bxu:obe:other:isaiah]], [[:bxu:obe:kt:jesus]], [[:bxu:obe:other:mary]])
